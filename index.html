Thankfulness

A game of reflection and gratitude.

How to play:

  • Enter the names of all of the players in your group.
  • When you are finished with entering the players hit "Start".
  • The game will randomly pick one player and one gratitude category.
  • The player must name one thing in that category that they are thankful for.
  • If the player cannot come up with something in that category, they can use their "Anything" option. In this case, the player can name anything that they are greatful for. Each player has one "Anything" option per game.
  • When a player completes their turn, hit "Next" and repeat.
  • You can add a time limit to each round if you would like, but it is not required. Time limits often lead to more hilarious outcomes.
